The honest answers

Will my answers be shared with my match?

No. Your survey responses are never shown to your match. Only your name and email are shared — and only once the full reveal happens.

The two-step reveal ♡

Email 1 (drop day, 8pm): initials + compatibility score. Sit with it. Email 2 (24 hours later): full name and Cambridge email. A little suspense before full contact.

How does email verification work?

Enter your @cam.ac.uk address and receive a magic link. Clicking it confirms you control that inbox — no passwords needed. Unverified entries are excluded from matching. One email = one entry.

What if I don't like my match?

You don't have to do anything. No obligation. But maybe give it a chance — the algorithm probably knows something you don't.

How does the matching algorithm work?

Every 1–7 answer is compared across all participants. We compute a similarity score for each possible pair, then run an optimal one-to-one matching algorithm — finding the best outcome for everyone simultaneously, not just locally good pairings.
